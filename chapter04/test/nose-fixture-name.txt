
主要是不同风格的命名 如驼峰,下划线 etc.


[Fixture]           [Alternative-Name]

setUpPackage            setup_package
tearDownPackage         teardown_package

setUpModule             setup_module 
tearDownModule          teardown_module

setUpClass              setupClass, setup_class, setupAll, setUpAll
tearDownClass           teardownClass, teardown_class, teardownAll, tearDownAll

setUp (class method)        setup
tearDown (class method)     teardown
